PROJECT NAME:Multi Channel marketing attribution 
PROJECT SCOPE
The Multi-Channel Marketing Attribution system is designed to evaluate the effectiveness of various digital marketing channels. Customers interact with multiple platforms such as email, social media, search ads, and display ads before making a purchase or conversion. This project helps businesses understand which channels play the most important role in customer conversion.

Objectives
Analyze customer journeys across channels
Measure channel contribution to conversions
Compare attribution models
Improve marketing decision-making
Optimize advertising budget allocation

Functional Scope
Dataset Management
Upload CSV datasets
Preview and validate data
Handle missing or incorrect values
Customer Journey Tracking
Track sequence of customer interactions
Generate complete journey paths
